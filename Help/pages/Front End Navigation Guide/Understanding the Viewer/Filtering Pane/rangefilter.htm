Range Filtering

    • Click add -> choose field -> adjust range values in filter pane using slider bar or manually typing values.
    • You can add multiple range filters on both columns and fields
    • Click Clear All Filters to remove your selections.
    • Click on Update 3D view to refresh the view to show only glyphs that match your filters or click Show Filtered Data, Create Subset Visualization, or Show Selected Data (see below for descriptions of these options).
