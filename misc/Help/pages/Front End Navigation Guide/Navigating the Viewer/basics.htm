The Basics

There are two ways of navigating the viewer: directly with your mouse, or using the on screen direction controls. The on screen controls (circled in yellow) can be found in the upper right hand corner of the visualization screen.




What You Want to Do: Using Your Mouse: Using Screen Controls: Screen Control View:
Zoom in and out Click a glyph on the screen -> hold down the R+L keys on the mouse and move the mouse forward or backwards Click a glyph on the screen -> use + or – button on screen
Rotate View Click anywhere on screen -> drag left or right Click aywhere on screen -> use ip, down, left, right arrows
Move the Screen Up/Down If these up/down arrows are greyed out, right click in the visual environment -> click “clear selection” Load visualization -> use up/down arrows under zoom keys
*If these up/down arrows are greyed out, go to the View drop down menu -> clear selection -> arrows will now be available for use
